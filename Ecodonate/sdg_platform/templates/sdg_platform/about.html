About EcoDonate

Bridging the gap between global goals and local action through transparent, community-driven crowdfunding.

Our Mission

EcoDonate was founded with a single vision: to empower Kenyans to contribute directly to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). We believe that small contributions, when channeled into well-managed community projects, can solve our greatest challenges—from poverty to climate change.

Our platform ensures that every KES donated goes directly toward the project’s targets, with real-time progress tracking for every donor.

Why Choose EcoDonate?

Transparency

Track exactly how much has been raised and see the impact of your donation in real-time.

SDG Aligned

Every project is tagged with a specific UN Sustainable Development Goal for clear impact reporting.

Local Support

We prioritize grassroots initiatives that understand the unique needs of Kenyan communities.
